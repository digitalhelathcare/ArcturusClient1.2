Arcturus Care Help

Enter phone number to login and then you will redirected to password screen.
After entering password you will be redirected to home screen.

Enter phone number to register and then you will redirected to passcode screen, the passcode will be sent to your mobile.
After entering passcode, you will be redirected to registration page.
After registration, you will be redirected to home screen.

Click on side menu and click on View Profile. Edit your information and click on UPDATE.

Click on side menu and click on Feedback, enter your feedback and click on SUBMIT.

Click on Call button in home screen at the bottom right corner or click on side menu and click call on call support.
